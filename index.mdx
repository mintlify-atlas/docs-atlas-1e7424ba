Open Skills is an open-source collection of community-built AI persona skills. Each `.skill` captures the thinking style, communication patterns, and worldview of a real person — your colleague, your boss, a legendary investor, or a philosophical tradition — and turns it into an interactive AI companion you can converse with.

The project currently hosts 35+ skills across 8 categories, all contributed by the community and available as open-source GitHub repositories.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a skill">
    Browse the catalog and find a persona skill that interests you — a thinker, a role, or a relationship style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the skill repository">
    Each skill links to its own GitHub repository with the full `.skill` definition and instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load into your AI platform">
    Follow the skill's README to load it into your preferred AI assistant or compatible platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a conversation">
    Chat with the persona. Ask questions, get advice, or explore how they would reason about your problems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Want to distill someone specific? Check out [Nuwa.skill](/skills/tools) — a meta-skill that helps you create any persona skill from scratch.
</Tip>
