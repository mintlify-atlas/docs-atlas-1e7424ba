The Workplace & Academia category contains skills distilled from the professional and academic people in your life. Whether you want to anticipate what your boss would prioritize in a meeting, get research guidance from your graduate supervisor at 2 a.m., or generate a perfectly bureaucratic rejection letter, these skills turn professional relationships into always-available AI companions. Each skill is built from real materials — chat histories, meeting notes, comments, and project documents — so the resulting persona reflects how that person actually thinks.

<Tip>
  **Practical use cases:**
  - Ask **Boss.skill** what your manager would prioritize before a quarterly review.
  - Ask **Supervisor.skill** for feedback on a draft methodology at any hour.
  - Use **Professor.skill** to generate likely exam topics based on lecture materials.
  - Use **HR.skill** to decode the real message in a vague performance review.
</Tip>

<Info>
  **Anti-Distill.skill** is a countermeasure for the distillation age. Companies are increasingly asking employees to encode their expertise into persona skills. Anti-Distill.skill generates a plausible-looking skill output that satisfies the request — without actually transferring your hard-won knowledge. Think of it as a corporate compliance layer for your brain.
</Info>
