---
title: "Self-Development Skills: distill yourself and your world"
description: "Skills for personal growth and self-reflection — distill your own thinking, build a digital legacy, or capture memorable life moments as AI personas."
---

Most persona skills distill someone else — a boss, a thinker, a historical figure. Self-Development skills turn the lens inward. They ask: what would it look like to distill *yourself*? Your reasoning patterns, your values, your way of seeing the world? This category also extends the idea beyond people: a pet, a place, a team, a season of your life — anything that shaped you can be preserved as a skill. These are tools for building a richer relationship with your own history and future self.

<Tip>
  **Yourself.skill** and **Immortal.skill** are the two flagship self-distillation skills. Start with Yourself.skill if you want to create an interactive version of your current thinking. Use Immortal.skill if your goal is to preserve something lasting — a version of yourself that others can learn from.

  **Relic.skill** is broader than it first appears. You can use it to capture not just people, but pets, relationships, places, teams, and specific moments in time. If something shaped who you are and you don't want to lose it to memory's drift, Relic.skill is the right tool.
</Tip>
