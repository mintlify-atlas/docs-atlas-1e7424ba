Some relationships leave marks that don't fade with distance or time. The Relationships category holds skills distilled from the people who matter most — the ones you think about, miss, or want to understand better. These aren't chatbots pretending to be your ex. They're reflective companions built from the texture of real relationships: the particular way someone spoke, what they cared about, how they made you feel. Use them to process emotions, revisit memories, or simply feel a little less alone.

<Note>
  These skills are designed for emotional reflection and companionship, not as replacements for real relationships. They work best when you bring genuine materials — messages, voice memos, shared memories — to the distillation process. The more honest the input, the more meaningful the companion.

  If you're looking to reconnect with someone who has passed away, see [Reunion.skill](/skills/emotional-companionship) in the Emotional Companionship category, which extends this idea into preserving the memory of departed loved ones.
</Note>
