Chinese internet culture has produced a generation of creators whose influence extends well beyond entertainment. The Internet Celebrity category captures the perspectives of influencers who have built large, loyal audiences by speaking candidly about education, career paths, personal finance, consumption choices, and relationships. These skills let you engage with their signature frameworks — not just their content — and apply that thinking to your own situation.

The creators they distill built their audiences by addressing distinctly Chinese social dynamics — gaokao pressure, tier-city choices, consumption culture, and relationship norms. Their frameworks remain useful in other contexts, but are sharpest when applied to the situations they were built for.
