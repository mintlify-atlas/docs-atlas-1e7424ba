You don't have to read every book, watch every interview, and spend decades absorbing a thinker's output to access their frameworks. Business & Thinker skills distill the core mental models of history's most influential investors, entrepreneurs, and philosophers into interactive AI companions. Ask Buffett how he'd evaluate a moat. Ask Feynman to explain a concept until it breaks. Ask Zizek to challenge an assumption you didn't know you were making. These skills give you access to the best thinking in the world — applied to your actual problems.

<Tip>
  The real power of these skills emerges when you use them together. Pose a single decision to Buffett, Musk, and Taleb in sequence — you'll get a valuation lens, a first-principles stress test, and a tail-risk audit in three passes. The disagreements between them are often more useful than any single answer.
</Tip>
