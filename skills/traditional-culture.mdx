<Info>
  **What is Bazi (Four Pillars)?**

  Bazi, also called the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a Chinese metaphysical system that maps a person's birth year, month, day, and hour onto a set of Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches. The resulting four "pillars" are analyzed against classical texts to reveal tendencies in personality, relationships, career, and timing. Fortune.skill automates the calculation and applies interpretations from nine foundational texts, making this traditionally complex system accessible through conversation.
</Info>
